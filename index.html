Axiom Congress Feedback Portal

  • My most recent project has been designing and developing the Congress Feedback Portal for Axiom Health Ltd.
  • The Portal is designed for gathering feedback from healthcare professionals (HCPs) at medical congresses.
  • Some screenshots of the portal are displayed to the left:
    1. HCPs sign in to the Portal with their own username and password
    2. The home page is tailored to each HCP
    3. They can see the list of congress abstracts they are requested to give feedback on, and click a link to take them to each individual survey, hosted on a separate survey platform. They can also toggle the Completed button when they have finished a survey.
  • As Axiom's sole developer, I designed and built this Portal from scratch using Bootstrap, Flask and PostgreSQL.
  • The Portal is hosted on Heroku and deployed with GitHub.
